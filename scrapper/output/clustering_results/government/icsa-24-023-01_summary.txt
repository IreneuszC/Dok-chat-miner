CISA is urging organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before implementing defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in APsystems products. The company has not responded to requests to address the vulnerability, and a proof of concept has been discovered. The vulnerability allows attackers to access sensitive data and execute commands with full admin rights. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and best practices to mitigate the risk. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to report any suspicious activity to CISA.
