CISA advises organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before implementing defensive measures. They recommend taking steps to minimize the risk of exploitation, as Integration Objects has not responded to requests to address vulnerabilities. Developers using affected versions of OPC UA Server Toolkit should contact Sam Hanson of Dragos, who reported the vulnerability to CISA. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3. CISA offers cyber defense best practices and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. They also provide guidance for observing and reporting suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting the vulnerability has been reported to CISA at this time.
