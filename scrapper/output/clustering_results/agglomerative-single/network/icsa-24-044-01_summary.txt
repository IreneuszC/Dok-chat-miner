Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in their MELSEC iQ-R Series Safety CPU and SIL2 Process CPU modules, which could allow a non-administrator user to disclose the credentials of a user with a lower access level. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 6.5 has been calculated for this vulnerability. Mitsubishi Electric recommends updating the CPU module and enabling enhanced version of vulnerability management in GX Works3 to prevent the attack. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established procedures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
