The article discusses a Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could lead to a major nonrecoverable fault. The company has reported the vulnerability to CISA and recommends applying risk mitigations and security best practices to minimize the risk. CISA also advises organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
