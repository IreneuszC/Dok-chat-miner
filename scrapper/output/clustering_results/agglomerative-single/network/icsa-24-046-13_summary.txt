Siemens has reported vulnerabilities to CISA and released new versions for affected products. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to crash applications, execute code, and perform remote code execution. Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations, and recommends protecting network access and configuring the environment according to policy. No known public exploitation has been reported, and organizations should follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage and in product manuals. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Additional information on industrial security by Siemens can be found on their website.
