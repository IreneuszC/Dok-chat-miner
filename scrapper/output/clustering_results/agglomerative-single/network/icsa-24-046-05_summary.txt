Siemens reported a vulnerability to CISA, which could allow an unauthenticated remote attacker to gain full administrative access to certain Siemens products. A CVSS v3 base score of 9.8 has been assigned to the vulnerability. Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations, and CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
