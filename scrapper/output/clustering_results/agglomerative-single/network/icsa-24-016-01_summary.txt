CISA has released additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices for a vulnerability in MOVITOOLS MotionStudio. No known public exploitation targeting the vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA and implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. The vulnerability, reported by Esjay working with Trend Micro Zero Day Initiative, has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.5. SEW-EURODRIVE is working to resolve the issue, and an advisory update will be released. Users of affected versions are advised to contact CISA for additional information.
