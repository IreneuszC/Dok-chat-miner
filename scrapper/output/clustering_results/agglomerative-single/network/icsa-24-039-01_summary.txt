CISA has reported a vulnerability in Qolsys IQ Panel 4 and IQ4 Hub versions prior to 4.4.2 panel software, which could allow unauthorized access to settings. No known public exploitation has been reported, and the vulnerability is not exploitable remotely. Organizations are encouraged to follow mitigation guidance and recommended practices provided by ICS and Johnson Controls. Defensive measures should be taken to minimize the risk of exploitation, and users are advised to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.3.
