Siemens has reported a vulnerability in the Treck TCP/IP stack on their devices to CISA. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could lead to denial of service or remote code execution. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Siemens has provided mitigation guidance and recommended practices to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
