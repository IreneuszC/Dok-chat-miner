Siemens has identified vulnerabilities in their products that could allow attackers to gain control of devices or cause denial of service. They recommend implementing security measures and following their product manuals for protection. Siemens has provided workarounds and mitigations for reducing risk, and has reported the vulnerabilities to CISA. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional information and mitigation guidance can be found on Siemens' industrial security webpage.
