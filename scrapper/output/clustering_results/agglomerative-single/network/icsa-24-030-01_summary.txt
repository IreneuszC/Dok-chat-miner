Emerson Rosemount GC370XA, GC700XA, and GC1500XA products have multiple vulnerabilities that could allow an unauthenticated user with network access to obtain sensitive information, cause a denial-of-service condition, and bypass authentication to acquire admin capabilities. Emerson recommends updating the affected products' firmware and implementing cybersecurity best practices. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported at this time.
