Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in Microsoft Message Queuing service affecting their electrical discharge machines, which could allow attackers to execute remote code, disclose, tamper with, destroy, or delete information, or cause a denial-of-service condition. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage, and users are advised to install the latest update and take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. Mitsubishi Electric has reported the vulnerability to CISA, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8 has been assigned to it.
