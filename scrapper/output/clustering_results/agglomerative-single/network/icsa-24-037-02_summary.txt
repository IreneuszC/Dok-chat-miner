HID Global has identified a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards that could allow sensitive data to be extracted, including credential and device administrator keys. They have provided steps for customers to update their readers and credentials with new keys, as well as offering a free upgrade to the Elite Key program. Customers are advised to keep their configuration cards secure and to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. CISA recommends organizations to follow established procedures and report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation of this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
