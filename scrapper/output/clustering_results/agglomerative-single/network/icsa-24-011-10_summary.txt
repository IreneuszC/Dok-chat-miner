Siemens has identified a vulnerability in the maxView Storage Manager on Windows, which could allow unauthorized access to devices. They have provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk, and have reported the vulnerability to CISA. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
