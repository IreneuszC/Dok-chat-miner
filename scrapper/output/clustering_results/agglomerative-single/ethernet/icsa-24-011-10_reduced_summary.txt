Siemens has identified a vulnerability in their maxView Storage Manager on Windows, which could allow unauthorized access to the system. To protect against this, Siemens recommends implementing appropriate network access mechanisms and configuring the IT environment according to their guidelines. Users are advised to apply specific workarounds and mitigations provided by Siemens to reduce the risk of exploitation. Additionally, CISA recommends implementing defensive measures and following established internal procedures to minimize the risk of exploitation.
