Siemens has reported a vulnerability in the Treck TCP/IP stack on their devices to CISA. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could lead to denial of service or remote code execution. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Siemens has provided workarounds and mitigations, and CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation.
