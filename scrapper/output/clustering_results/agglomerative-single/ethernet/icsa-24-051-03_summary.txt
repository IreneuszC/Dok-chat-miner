Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in Microsoft Message Queuing service that affects their electrical discharge machines, potentially allowing for remote code execution and tampering with or deleting information. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to follow cybersecurity strategies and implement recommended mitigations. Mitsubishi Electric recommends installing the latest update and has reported the vulnerability to CISA, which has assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8 to the vulnerability.
