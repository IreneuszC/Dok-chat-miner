Siemens products are affected by multiple vulnerabilities that could allow an attacker to execute code in the context of the current process by using specially crafted PAR files. The vulnerabilities have been assigned a CVSS v3 base score of 7.8 and are not exploitable remotely. Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk, and CISA recommends implementing recommended cybersecurity strategies and following established internal procedures to report any suspected malicious activity. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
