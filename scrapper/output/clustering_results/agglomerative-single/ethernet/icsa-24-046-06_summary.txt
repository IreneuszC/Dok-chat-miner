Siemens has identified a vulnerability in its products that could allow an attacker to gain SYSTEM privileges. The company has recommended protective measures and workarounds to reduce the risk of exploitation. CISA has been informed of the vulnerability and has assigned a base score of 7.8 and 8.5 to it. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Organizations are advised to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional information and mitigation guidance can be found on the ICS webpage.
