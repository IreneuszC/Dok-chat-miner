Siemens reported vulnerabilities to CISA and released updates for affected products. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to crash applications, execute code, and perform remote code execution. Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations, and recommends protecting network access and configuring the environment according to policy. No known public exploitation has been reported, and organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage and in product manuals. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and can find more information on industrial security by Siemens on their website.
