Siemens has identified vulnerabilities in their products that could allow attackers to gain unauthorized access and control of devices. They recommend implementing security measures and following their product manuals for protection. Siemens has reported the vulnerabilities to CISA and has provided workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk. No known public exploitation of these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. Additional information and mitigation guidance can be found on the Siemens and CISA websites.
