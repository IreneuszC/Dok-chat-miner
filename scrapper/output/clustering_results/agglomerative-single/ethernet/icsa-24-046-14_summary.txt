CISA has reported vulnerabilities in Siemens products, including weak file and folder permissions in Siemens Polarion ALM and lack of proper authentication in the REST API endpoints of doorsconnector. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Siemens is preparing fix versions and recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and recommended practices. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. Users can apply specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce risk.
