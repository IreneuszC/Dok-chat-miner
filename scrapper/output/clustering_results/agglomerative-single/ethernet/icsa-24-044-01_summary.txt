Mitsubishi Electric has reported an information disclosure vulnerability in the MELSEC iQ-R Series Safety CPU and SIL2 Process CPU modules, which could allow a non-administrator user to access the credentials of a user with lower access levels. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 6.5 has been calculated for this vulnerability. Mitsubishi Electric recommends enabling enhanced version of vulnerability management in GX Works3 to prevent the attack. Users are advised to contact their local Mitsubishi Electric representative for updates and to follow recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation of this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
