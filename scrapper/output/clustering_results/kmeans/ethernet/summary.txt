Multiple vulnerabilities have been reported in various industrial control systems and products, including those from Mitsubishi Electric, HID Global, Delta Electronics, Rockwell Automation, and others. These vulnerabilities could allow for unauthorized access, privilege escalation, remote code execution, and denial-of-service attacks. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, and organizations are encouraged to report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported at this time.
