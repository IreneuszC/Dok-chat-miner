A Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability has been identified in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could lead to a major nonrecoverable fault. Rockwell Automation recommends applying risk mitigations and implementing security best practices to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA advises organizations to take defensive measures and implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to protect against this vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
