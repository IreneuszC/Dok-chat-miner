Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in their MELSEC iQ-R Series Safety CPU and SIL2 Process CPU modules, which could allow a non-administrator user to disclose the credentials of a user with a lower access level. A workaround is available for some versions, and Mitsubishi Electric recommends updating the CPU module and implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
