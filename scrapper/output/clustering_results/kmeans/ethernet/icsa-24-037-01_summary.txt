CISA recommends users to take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in HID products, including hardening reader and encoder configurations, protecting credentials, and disabling legacy technologies. The vulnerability could expose sensitive data and allow attackers to read data from reader configuration cards and credentials. HID advises users to mitigate these threats and has reported the vulnerability to CISA. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
