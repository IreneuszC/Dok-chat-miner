CISA is urging organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to mitigate a privilege escalation vulnerability in FactoryTalk Service Platform (FTSP) reported by Rockwell Automation. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could allow malicious users to gain administrator group privileges, potentially compromising sensitive data and rendering the FTSP system unavailable. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but CISA recommends users take defensive measures and apply risk mitigations. Rockwell Automation has reported the vulnerability to CISA and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.0 has been calculated.
