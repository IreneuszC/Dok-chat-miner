HID Global has identified a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards that could allow sensitive data to be extracted, including credential and device administrator keys. They have provided steps for customers to update their readers and credentials with new keys, and are offering a free upgrade to the Elite Key program. Customers are advised to keep their configuration cards secure and take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and reporting any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported at this time, and it is not exploitable remotely.
