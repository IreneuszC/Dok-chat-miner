The article discusses a privilege escalation vulnerability in FactoryTalk Service Platform (FTSP) which could allow malicious users to gain administrator privileges and potentially access and modify sensitive data. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and mitigation guidance to minimize the risk of exploitation. Rockwell Automation encourages users to apply risk mitigations and implement their recommended practices. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.0.
