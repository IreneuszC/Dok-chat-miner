CISA recommends users to take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in HID products, including hardening reader modules and processors, protecting credentials and disabling legacy technologies, and protecting reader configuration cards. The vulnerability has a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.9 and could expose sensitive data when reader configuration cards are programmed. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. HID Global reported the vulnerability to CISA and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
