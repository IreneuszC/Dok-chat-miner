The Chirp Access policy allows an attacker to gain unrestricted physical access to systems and improperly stores credentials, potentially exposing sensitive information. No known public exploitation has been reported, but a high base score of 9.1 has been assigned to this vulnerability. Chirp Systems has not responded to requests to mitigate the issue. Users are encouraged to contact support for more information and take defensive measures. Organizations should implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA.
