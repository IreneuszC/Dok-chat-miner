Multiple vulnerabilities have been reported in various industrial control and automation products, including those from Mitsubishi Electric, Crestron, HID Global, SystemK, Delta Electronics, Rockwell Automation, Integration Objects, Lantronix, and AVEVA. These vulnerabilities could allow for unauthorized access, privilege escalation, information disclosure, remote code execution, and denial-of-service attacks. CISA recommends implementing defensive measures and cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported at this time.
