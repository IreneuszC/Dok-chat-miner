Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in multiple FA products to CISA. They recommend users to take defensive measures and implement cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time. The affected products include the Mitsubishi Electric MELSEC iQ-F Series, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 has been calculated for the vulnerability.
