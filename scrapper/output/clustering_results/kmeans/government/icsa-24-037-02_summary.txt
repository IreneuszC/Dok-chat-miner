HID Global has reported a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards, which could allow sensitive data to be extracted. They have provided steps for customers to update their readers and credentials with new keys, and are offering a free upgrade to the Elite Key program. Customers are advised to keep their configuration cards secure and to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. CISA recommends organizations to follow established procedures and report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely.
