Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in their MELSEC iQ-R Series products that could allow a non-administrator user to disclose the credentials of a user with lower access level. To prevent this, users should update their CPU module and enable communication with only the enhanced version of vulnerability management of GX Works3 when writing user information. It is recommended to contact a local Mitsubishi Electric representative for the update and to implement cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
