CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and follow established procedures for reporting suspected malicious activity. They also recommend users protect themselves from social engineering attacks and take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in Rockwell Automation products. No known public exploitation targeting the vulnerability has been reported, but if exploited, it could allow an attacker to retrieve user information and modify settings without authentication. Rockwell Automation has reported the vulnerability to CISA.
