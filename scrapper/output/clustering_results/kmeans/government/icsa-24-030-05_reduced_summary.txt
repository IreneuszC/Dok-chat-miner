The article discusses a Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could lead to a major nonrecoverable fault. Rockwell Automation recommends applying risk mitigations and implementing security best practices to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA also recommends defensive measures and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
