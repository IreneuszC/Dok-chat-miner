Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in Microsoft Message Queuing service affecting their electrical discharge machines, which could allow for remote code execution and potential tampering with or destruction of information. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to follow cybersecurity strategies and implement recommended mitigations. Mitsubishi Electric recommends installing the latest update and has reported the vulnerability to CISA, with a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8 assigned to it.
