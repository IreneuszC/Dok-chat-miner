Mitsubishi Electric has reported an authentication bypass vulnerability in the MELSEC WS Series Ethernet Interface Modules to CISA. The vulnerability allows remote attackers to bypass authentication and potentially disclose or tamper with programs and parameters in the modules. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.9 has been assigned to this vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
