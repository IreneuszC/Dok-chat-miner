Mitsubishi Electric has reported an information disclosure vulnerability in the MELSEC iQ-R Series Safety CPU and SIL2 Process CPU modules. A non-administrator user can potentially disclose the credentials of a user with lower access level by sending a specially crafted packet. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 6.5 has been calculated for this vulnerability. Mitsubishi Electric recommends updating the CPU module and enabling enhanced version of vulnerability management in GX Works3 to prevent the attack. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established procedures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
