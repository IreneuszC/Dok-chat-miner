CISA recommends organizations to perform impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities. Integration Objects has not responded to requests to mitigate vulnerabilities, and developers using affected versions of OPC UA Server Toolkit are advised to contact Sam Hanson of Dragos. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 has been calculated for the vulnerability, and several CISA products detailing cyber defense best practices are available for reading and download. Organizations observing suspected malicious activity should report findings to CISA, and no known public exploitation specifically targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
