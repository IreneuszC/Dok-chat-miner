CISA is urging organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before implementing defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in APsystems products. The company has not responded to requests to address the vulnerability, and a proof of concept has been discovered. The vulnerability allows attackers to access sensitive data and execute commands with full admin rights. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established procedures for reporting suspicious activity. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported at this time.
