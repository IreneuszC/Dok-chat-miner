CISA recommends performing impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures for various vulnerabilities in different products. Companies such as Crestron, Schneider Electric, SystemK, Lynx, APsystems, AVEVA, Horner Automation, MOVITOOLS MotionStudio, ViewPower Pro, Integration Objects, and Siemens have all been affected by vulnerabilities. CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and organizations are advised to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
