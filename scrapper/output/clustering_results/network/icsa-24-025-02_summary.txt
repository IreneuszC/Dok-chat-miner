CISA has discovered a command injection vulnerability in SystemK NVR 504/508/516 versions 2.3.5SK.30084998 and prior, which could allow attackers to execute arbitrary commands with root privileges. CISA recommends performing impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures, and encourages users to take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. SystemK has not responded to requests to mitigate the vulnerability, and CISA has provided guidance and best practices for cybersecurity. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are advised to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
