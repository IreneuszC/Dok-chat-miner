A Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability has been identified in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could potentially cause a major nonrecoverable fault (MNRF) and crash the device. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.6, and Rockwell Automation has reported it to CISA. Users are encouraged to apply risk mitigations and security best practices, and CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage. Organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
