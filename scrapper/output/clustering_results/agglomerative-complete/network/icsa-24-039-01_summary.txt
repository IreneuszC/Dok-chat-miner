CISA has reported a vulnerability in Qolsys IQ Panel 4 and IQ4 Hub versions prior to 4.4.2 panel software that could allow unauthorized access to settings. No known public exploitation has been reported, and the vulnerability is not exploitable remotely. Organizations are encouraged to follow mitigation guidance and recommended practices provided by ICS and implement cybersecurity strategies. Defensive measures should be taken to minimize the risk of exploitation, and Johnson Controls has provided recommendations to reduce the vulnerability. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.3.
