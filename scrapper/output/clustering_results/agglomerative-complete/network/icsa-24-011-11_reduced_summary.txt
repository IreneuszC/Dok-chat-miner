The article discusses multiple vulnerabilities in applications related to parsing specially crafted PAR files, which could allow attackers to execute code in the context of the current process. The vulnerabilities have been assigned a CVSS v3 base score of 7.8. To protect against these vulnerabilities, organizations are advised to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies, protect network access to devices, and follow the product manuals' recommendations. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
