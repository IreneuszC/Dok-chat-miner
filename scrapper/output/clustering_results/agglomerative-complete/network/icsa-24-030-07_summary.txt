Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Rockwell Automation products, with base scores ranging from 6.5 to 8.8. These vulnerabilities can be exploited through specifically crafted communication requests, leading to denial-of-service conditions, memory overwriting, or remote code execution. Rockwell Automation has recommended risk mitigations and encourages users to apply them. CISA has not received reports of public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities, but organizations are advised to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional information and mitigation guidance are available on the ICS webpage.
