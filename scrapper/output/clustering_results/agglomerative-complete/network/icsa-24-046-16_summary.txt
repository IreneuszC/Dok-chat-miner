CISA is urging organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to mitigate a privilege escalation vulnerability in FactoryTalk Service Platform (FTSP) reported by Rockwell Automation. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could allow malicious users to gain administrator group privileges, potentially leading to data manipulation and system unavailability. No known public exploitation has been reported, but CISA recommends defensive measures and risk mitigations. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.0.
