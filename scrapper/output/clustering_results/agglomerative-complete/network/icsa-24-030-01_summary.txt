Emerson Rosemount GC370XA, GC700XA, and GC1500XA products have multiple vulnerabilities that could allow an unauthenticated user with network access to obtain sensitive information, cause a denial-of-service condition, and bypass authentication to acquire admin capabilities. Emerson recommends updating the affected products' firmware and implementing cybersecurity best practices. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. CVSS v3.1 base scores have been calculated for the vulnerabilities, with the highest score being 9.8.
