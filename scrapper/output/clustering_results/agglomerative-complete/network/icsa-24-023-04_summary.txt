CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to mitigate reported vulnerabilities in Westermo products. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to introduce arbitrary code, execute malicious code, obtain sensitive information, or execute malicious requests. The affected products include Lynx 206-F2G industrial Ethernet switches. The vulnerabilities have been reported to CISA by Aarn Flecha Menndez, Ivn Alonso lvarez, and Vctor Bello Cuevas. The vulnerabilities have been assigned CVSS scores and vector strings, and potential attackers could exploit the vulnerabilities to carry out malicious actions or obtain sensitive information.
