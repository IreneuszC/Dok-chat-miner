Siemens has identified a vulnerability in its products that could allow an attacker to gain SYSTEM privileges. They have recommended protective measures and workarounds to reduce the risk of exploitation. CISA has been informed of the vulnerability and has assigned a base score. No known public exploitation has been reported, and the vulnerability is not exploitable remotely. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
