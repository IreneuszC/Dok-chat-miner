CISA recommends users to upgrade to AVEVA Edge 2023 or AVEVA Edge 2020 R2 SP2 P01 to minimize the risk of exploitation of the vulnerability. The vulnerability could allow a malicious entity to achieve arbitrary code execution and privilege escalation by tricking AVEVA Edge to load an unsafe DLL. Organizations should implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and take measures to protect themselves from social engineering attacks. No known public exploitation specifically targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
