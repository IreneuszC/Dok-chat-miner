Siemens has issued a security advisory for a vulnerability in maxView Storage Manager on Windows. The vulnerability could allow unauthorized access to the system. Siemens recommends implementing appropriate security measures and following their recommendations in product manuals. CISA has been notified and organizations are advised to take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations should report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
