Multiple Siemens products are affected by vulnerabilities, including null pointer dereference, stack overflow, and buffer overflow vulnerabilities while parsing specially crafted SPP, WRL, and PSOBJ files. These vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to crash the application, execute arbitrary code, or cause denial of service. Siemens recommends defensive measures and implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and they are not exploitable remotely. Siemens has identified specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk, and users are advised to follow the recommendations in the product manuals and protect network access to devices with appropriate mechanisms.
