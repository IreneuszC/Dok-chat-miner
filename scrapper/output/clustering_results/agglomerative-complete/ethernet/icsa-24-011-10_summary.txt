Siemens has issued a security advisory for the vulnerability in maxView Storage Manager on Windows. They recommend protecting network access and configuring the IT environment according to their recommendations. Siemens has identified specific workarounds and mitigations for reducing risk. CISA has been notified of the vulnerability and recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations should report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
