An authentication bypass vulnerability exists in the MELSEC WS Series Ethernet Interface Modules, allowing remote unauthenticated attackers to login and potentially disclose or tamper with programs and parameters. Mitsubishi Electric recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established internal procedures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
