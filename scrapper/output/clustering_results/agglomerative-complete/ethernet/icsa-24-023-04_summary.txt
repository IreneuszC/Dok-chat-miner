CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and best practices to mitigate reported vulnerabilities in Westermo's Lynx 206-F2G industrial Ethernet switch. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to execute malicious code, access sensitive information, and carry out social engineering attacks. CISA also advises organizations to report any suspected malicious activity and offers additional mitigation guidance on their website. Multiple vulnerabilities have been assigned CVSS scores, with the highest being 8.0 for the potential execution of malicious code on the device. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported to CISA at this time.
