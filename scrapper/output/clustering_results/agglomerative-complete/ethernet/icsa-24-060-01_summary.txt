Delta Electronics products, specifically CNCSoft-B versions 1.0.0.4 and prior, are vulnerable to a stack-based buffer overflow which could allow an attacker to execute arbitrary code. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Organizations should follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.8 has been assigned to this vulnerability, and users are encouraged to update to the latest version. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation.
