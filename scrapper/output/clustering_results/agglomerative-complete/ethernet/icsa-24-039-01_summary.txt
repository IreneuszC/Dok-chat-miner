CISA has reported a vulnerability in Qolsys IQ Panel 4 and IQ4 Hub versions prior to 4.4.2 panel software, which could allow unauthorized access to settings. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and taking defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. Johnson Controls has provided recommendations to reduce the vulnerability risk, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.3 has been calculated for this vulnerability.
