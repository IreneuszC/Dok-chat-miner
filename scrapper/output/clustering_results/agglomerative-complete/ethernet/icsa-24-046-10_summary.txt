Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Siemens products, specifically related to parsing specially crafted Catia MODEL files. These vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to execute code in the context of the current process. Siemens has reported these vulnerabilities to CISA and has provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and they are not exploitable remotely. CISA recommends implementing recommended cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. Additionally, Siemens recommends protecting network access to devices and following recommendations in the product manuals.
