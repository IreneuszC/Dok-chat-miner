The article discusses multiple vulnerabilities in applications related to parsing specially crafted PAR files, which could allow attackers to execute code in the context of the current process. The vulnerabilities have been assigned a CVSS v3 base score of 7.8. To protect against these vulnerabilities, organizations are advised to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies, protect network access to devices, and follow established internal procedures for reporting suspected malicious activity. Siemens has also provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk of exploitation.
