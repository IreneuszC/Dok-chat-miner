Siemens has reported vulnerabilities in their products and recommends updating to the latest versions to protect against potential attacks. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to crash the application, execute code, or perform remote code execution. Siemens recommends protecting network access and configuring the environment according to their security policy. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations should follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. It is also advised not to open untrusted XT files in Parasolid.
