Siemens has reported vulnerabilities to CISA and released new versions for affected products. The vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to crash the application, execute code, or perform remote code execution. Siemens has assigned CVSS scores to the vulnerabilities and recommends updating to the latest versions of affected products. Specific workarounds and mitigations are also provided, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and organizations are advised to follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage and in product manuals.
