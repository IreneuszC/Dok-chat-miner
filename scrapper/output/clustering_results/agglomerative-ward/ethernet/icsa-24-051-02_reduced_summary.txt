The Industrial Control Systems Network Protocol Parsers (ICSNPP) - Ethercat Zeek Plugin has vulnerabilities that could allow an attacker to crash the system, leak information, and execute arbitrary code. Users are advised to update the plugin and keep software patches up to date to reduce the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations should follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
