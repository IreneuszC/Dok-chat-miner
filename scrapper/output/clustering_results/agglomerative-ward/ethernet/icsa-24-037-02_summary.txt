HID Global has identified a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards that could allow sensitive data to be extracted, including credential and device administrator keys. They have provided steps for customers to update their readers and credentials with new keys, and are offering a free upgrade to the Elite Key program. Customers are advised to keep their configuration cards secure and to restrict access to them. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of this vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. The vulnerability could allow an attacker to read the keys from a configuration card, which could be used to create malicious configuration cards or credentials.
