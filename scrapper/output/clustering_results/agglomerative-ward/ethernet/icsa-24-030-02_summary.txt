Mitsubishi Electric has reported vulnerabilities in their FA Engineering Software Products, which could allow remote attackers to bypass authentication, disclose or tamper with information, and execute malicious code. The company recommends mitigation measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
