The article discusses a Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could lead to a major nonrecoverable fault. The recommended solution is for users to apply risk mitigations and implement security best practices to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA also recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
