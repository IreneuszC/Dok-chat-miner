The Crestron AirMedia Presentation System products are affected by an OS command injection vulnerability in the AM-300 firmware version 1.4499.00018, which could allow a user to escalate their privileges to root-level access. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.4 and has been reported to CISA by Uri Katz of Claroty Research Team. Crestron has resolved the vulnerability in firmware version 1.4499.00023.001 or higher. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
