A Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability has been identified in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could potentially cause the device to crash. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.6, and Rockwell Automation has reported it to CISA. Users are encouraged to apply risk mitigations and implement security best practices to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are advised to follow recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA.
