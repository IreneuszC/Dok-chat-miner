The article discusses a vulnerability in HID Global's iCLASS SE reader configuration cards, which could allow attackers to extract sensitive data such as credential and device administrator keys. To mitigate this risk, administrators are advised to update their readers and credentials with new keys, destroy unneeded configuration cards, and restrict access to configuration cards. Additionally, organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. The vulnerability is not exploitable remotely, but users should take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation.
