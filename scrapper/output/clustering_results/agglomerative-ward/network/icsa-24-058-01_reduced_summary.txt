Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in their FA products, which could be exploited by a remote attacker to cause temporary disruption in Ethernet communication. To minimize the risk, users are advised to implement defensive measures and update their systems as per the recommendations provided by CISA. Organizations should also follow cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
