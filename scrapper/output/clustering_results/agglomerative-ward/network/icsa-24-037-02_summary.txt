HID Global has identified a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards that could allow sensitive data to be extracted, including credential and device administrator keys. They have provided steps for customers to update their readers and credentials with new keys, and are offering a free upgrade to the Elite Key program. Customers are advised to keep their configuration cards secure and to restrict access to them. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established procedures for reporting suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely.
