Siemens has identified vulnerabilities in their products, including an infinite loop in OPC UA .NET Standard Stack 1.04.368 and issues with SSL_check_chain() function in OpenSSL. These vulnerabilities could allow remote attackers to cause denial-of-service attacks. Siemens has released a new version of SIDIS Prime and recommends users update to the latest version. They also recommend protecting network access to devices and configuring the environment according to product manuals. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
