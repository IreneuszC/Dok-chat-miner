CISA recommends organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and follow established procedures for reporting suspected malicious activity. Users are advised to protect themselves from social engineering attacks and Rockwell Automation products are affected by a vulnerability that could allow unauthorized access and modification of settings. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8 has been calculated for this vulnerability and Rockwell Automation has reported it to CISA. Defensive measures are recommended to minimize the risk of exploitation.
