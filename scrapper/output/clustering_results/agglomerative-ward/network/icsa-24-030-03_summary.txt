Mitsubishi Electric has reported an authentication bypass vulnerability in the MELSEC WS Series Ethernet Interface Modules to CISA. The vulnerability allows remote unauthenticated attackers to bypass authentication and login to the modules, potentially disclosing or tampering with programs and parameters. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.9 has been assigned to this vulnerability. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
