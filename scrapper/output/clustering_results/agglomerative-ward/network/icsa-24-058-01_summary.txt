Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in multiple FA products to CISA. They recommend mitigation measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, including implementing cybersecurity strategies and reporting any suspicious activity. The vulnerability affects certain versions of the MELSEC iQ-F Series and could be exploited by a remote attacker to cause a temporary denial-of-service condition in the product's Ethernet communication. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
