The APsystems ENERGY COMMUNICATION UNIT (ECU-C) Power Control Software V4.1NA V3.11.4 W2.1NA V4.1SAA C1.2.2 has a vulnerability that allows attackers to access sensitive data and execute commands with full admin rights. APsystems has not responded to requests to mitigate the vulnerability, and users are encouraged to contact CISA for assistance. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.8 has been calculated for this vulnerability, and no known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported to CISA at this time.
