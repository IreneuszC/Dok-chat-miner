CISA recommends users take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in the FeverWarn ecosystem, an IoT-based skin temperature scanning system. These vulnerabilities include insufficiently protected cloud integration, unchangeable credentials, unprotected API, unauthenticated messaging, and configuration issues. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and the FeverWarn product has been discontinued. Users are encouraged to follow mitigation guidance and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
