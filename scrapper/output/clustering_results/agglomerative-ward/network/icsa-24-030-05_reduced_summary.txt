The article discusses a Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could lead to a major nonrecoverable fault. The recommended security measures include applying risk mitigations, implementing suggested security best practices, and following defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
