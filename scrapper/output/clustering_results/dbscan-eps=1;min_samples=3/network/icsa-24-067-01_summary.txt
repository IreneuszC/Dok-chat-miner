Chirp Access has a vulnerability that could allow attackers to gain unrestricted physical access to systems and expose sensitive information. The vulnerability has a base score of 9.1 and has been reported to CISA, but no public exploitation has been reported. Chirp Systems has not responded to requests to mitigate the vulnerability. Users are encouraged to contact support for more information and take defensive measures. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and reporting any suspicious activity.
