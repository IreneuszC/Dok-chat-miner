Siemens has issued a security advisory for the vulnerability in maxView Storage Manager on Windows. They recommend protecting network access to devices and configuring the IT environment according to their recommendations. Siemens has identified specific workarounds and mitigations for reducing risk. The vulnerability has been reported to CISA and a CVSS v3 base score of 10.0 has been assigned. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
