Siemens provides industrial security recommendations in their product manuals and security advisories. They recommend protecting network access to devices and configuring the IT environment according to their guidelines. A vulnerability in the WebSocket channel used for the Check Point IoT integration in Nozomi Networks Guardian and CMC has been reported, and Siemens has notified CISA. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
