CISA has issued a technical information paper encouraging organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in Siemens products. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are advised to take defensive measures and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations for users to reduce the risk, and additional information on industrial security can be found on their website.
