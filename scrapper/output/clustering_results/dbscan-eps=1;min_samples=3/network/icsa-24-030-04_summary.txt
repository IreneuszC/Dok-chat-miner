CISA has recommended defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in Hitron Systems DVR. They have also received reports of active exploitation of these vulnerabilities and have assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.4 to each vulnerability. Hitron Systems has released updates for their DVR to address the vulnerabilities. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Aline Eliovich, Chad Seaman, and Larry Cashdollar of Akamai Technologies reported the vulnerabilities to CISA.
