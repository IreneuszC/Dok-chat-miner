Siemens has identified vulnerabilities in their products that could allow attackers to gain control of devices or cause denial of service. They recommend protecting network access and configuring the environment according to their recommendations. Siemens has reported the vulnerabilities to CISA and has provided workarounds and mitigations for users to reduce the risk. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional information and mitigation guidance can be found on Siemens' industrial security webpage.
