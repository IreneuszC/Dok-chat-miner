The ICS webpage provides additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices for a vulnerability in MOVITOOLS MotionStudio. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported to CISA at this time, and it is not exploitable remotely. Organizations observing suspicious activity should report findings to CISA. CISA encourages implementing cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. The vulnerability has been reported to CISA and a base score of 5.5 has been calculated. SEW-EURODRIVE is working to resolve the issue, and an advisory update will be forthcoming.
