A vulnerability in Crestron AirMedia Presentation System products could allow an attacker to escalate their privileges to root-level access. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.4 and has been resolved in firmware version 1.4499.00023.001 or higher. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely.
