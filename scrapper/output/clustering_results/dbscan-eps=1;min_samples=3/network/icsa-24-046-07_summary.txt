Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Siemens products, including null pointer dereference, stack overflow, and buffer overflow vulnerabilities while parsing specially crafted SPP, WRL, and PSOBJ files. These vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to crash the application, execute arbitrary code, or cause denial of service conditions. Siemens has assigned CVSS v3 and v4 base scores to these vulnerabilities and recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and Siemens has provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce risk. CISA recommends implementing recommended cybersecurity strategies and reporting any suspected malicious activity. Additionally, Siemens recommends protecting network access to devices and configuring the environment according to their security recommendations.
