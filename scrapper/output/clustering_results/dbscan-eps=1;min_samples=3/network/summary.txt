Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in various industrial products from companies such as Rockwell Automation, Mitsubishi Electric, Crestron, Siemens, HID Global, Delta Electronics, AVEVA, Chirp Access, APsystems, Hitron Systems, and Nice Linear. These vulnerabilities range from denial-of-service conditions to remote code execution and privilege escalation. The companies have provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk, and CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
