Siemens has reported vulnerabilities to CISA and released new versions for affected products. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to crash applications, execute code, and perform remote code execution. Siemens has provided workarounds and mitigation recommendations, and advises protecting network access and configuring devices in a protected IT environment. No known public exploitation has been reported, and organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage and in product manuals. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and can find additional information on industrial security by Siemens on their website.
