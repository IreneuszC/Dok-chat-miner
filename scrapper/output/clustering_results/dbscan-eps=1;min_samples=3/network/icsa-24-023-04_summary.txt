CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and best practices to mitigate reported vulnerabilities in Lynx 206-F2G industrial Ethernet switches. The vulnerabilities could allow attackers to inject arbitrary code, execute malicious actions, and obtain sensitive information. CISA also advises protection against social engineering attacks and encourages reporting of suspicious activity. The vulnerabilities have been reported by Aarn Flecha Menndez, Ivn Alonso lvarez, and Vctor Bello Cuevas. The CVSS v3.1 base scores for the vulnerabilities range from 5.4 to 8.0.
