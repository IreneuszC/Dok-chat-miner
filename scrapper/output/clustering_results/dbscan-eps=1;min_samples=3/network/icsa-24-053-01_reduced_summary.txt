The article discusses a vulnerability in Delta Electronics products that could allow for remote code execution. CISA recommends organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and follow internal procedures to report any suspicious activity. Users are advised to take defensive measures to protect themselves from social engineering attacks and to upgrade to the latest version of the affected software. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.8 and organizations are encouraged to visit the ICS webpage for additional mitigation guidance.
