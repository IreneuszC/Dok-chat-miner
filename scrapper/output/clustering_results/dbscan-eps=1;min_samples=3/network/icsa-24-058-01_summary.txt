Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in multiple FA products to CISA. They recommend specific mitigation measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, and CISA encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity. The vulnerability allows for a temporary denial-of-service condition in the product's Ethernet communication through a TCP SYN Flood attack. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 has been calculated.
