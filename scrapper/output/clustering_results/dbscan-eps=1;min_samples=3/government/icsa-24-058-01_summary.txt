Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in multiple FA products, specifically in the Ethernet function. The vulnerability could be exploited by a remote attacker to cause a temporary denial-of-service condition through a TCP SYN Flood attack. CISA recommends implementing defensive measures and cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 has been calculated. Mitsubishi Electric has provided specific update instructions and additional details for mitigation.
