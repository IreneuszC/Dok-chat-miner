Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Siemens products, allowing attackers to execute code in the context of the current process by using specially crafted PAR files. The vulnerabilities have been assigned a CVSS v3 base score of 7.8 and are not exploitable remotely. Siemens has provided workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk, and CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established internal procedures to protect against these vulnerabilities.
