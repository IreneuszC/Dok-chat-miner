Siemens reported a vulnerability to CISA, which could allow an unauthenticated remote attacker to gain full administrative access to certain Siemens products. The CVSS v3 base score is 9.8, and additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
