The Industrial Control Systems Network Protocol Parsers (ICSNPP) - Ethercat Zeek Plugin versions d78dda6 and prior are vulnerable to out-of-bounds read, out-of-bounds write, and remote code execution. Users are advised to update the plugin and implement defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
