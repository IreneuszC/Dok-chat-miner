Siemens has reported vulnerabilities to CISA and released new versions for affected products. The vulnerabilities could allow for denial of service, remote code execution, and out of bounds read past the end of an allocated structure. Siemens recommends updating to the latest versions and not opening untrusted XT files in Parasolid. Specific workarounds and mitigations are provided, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
