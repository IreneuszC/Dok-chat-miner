The article discusses multiple vulnerabilities in applications related to parsing specially crafted PAR files, which could allow attackers to execute code in the context of the current process. The vulnerabilities have been assigned a CVSS v3 base score of 7.8 and are not exploitable remotely. To protect against these vulnerabilities, organizations are advised to follow established internal procedures, report findings to CISA, and implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Siemens has also provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk of exploitation.
