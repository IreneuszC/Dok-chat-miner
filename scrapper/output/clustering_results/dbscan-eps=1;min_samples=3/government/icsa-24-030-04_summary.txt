CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in Hitron Systems DVR. The vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to cause a denial-of-service condition using default admin name and password. Hitron Systems has released updates for affected DVR versions. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.4 has been calculated for the vulnerabilities. Organizations should report suspected malicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
