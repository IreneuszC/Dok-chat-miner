Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Siemens products, specifically related to parsing specially crafted Catia MODEL files. These vulnerabilities could allow an attacker to execute code in the context of the current process. Siemens has reported these vulnerabilities to CISA and has provided specific workarounds and mitigations for users to reduce the risk. CISA recommends implementing recommended cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation specifically targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported at this time.
