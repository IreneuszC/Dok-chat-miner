CISA recommends users to take defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in HID products, including hardening readers and protecting credentials. The vulnerability could expose sensitive data and allow attackers to read data from reader configuration cards and credentials. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. HID Global reported the vulnerability to CISA and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
