Siemens provides industrial security recommendations in product manuals and security advisories. A vulnerability in the WebSocket channel used for the Check Point IoT integration in Nozomi Networks Guardian and CMC may allow an unauthenticated attacker to obtain assets data. Siemens reported this vulnerability to CISA and recommends defensive measures. No known public exploitation has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to implement cybersecurity strategies. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
