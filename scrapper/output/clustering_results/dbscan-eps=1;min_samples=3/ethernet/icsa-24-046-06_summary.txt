Siemens has identified a vulnerability in their products that could allow an attacker to gain SYSTEM privileges. They have reported this to CISA and provided workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk. CISA recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
