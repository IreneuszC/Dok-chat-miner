Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in their FA products to CISA. They recommend users to take defensive measures and implement cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 has been calculated. The vulnerability exists in the Ethernet function of certain Mitsubishi Electric MELSEC iQ-F Series products, and a remote attacker could cause a temporary denial-of-service condition by performing a TCP SYN Flood attack. Products with * are sold in limited regions.
