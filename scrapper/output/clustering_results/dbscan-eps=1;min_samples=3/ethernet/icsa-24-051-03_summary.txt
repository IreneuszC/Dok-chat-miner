Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in Microsoft Message Queuing service that affects their electrical discharge machines, potentially allowing for remote code execution and tampering with or deleting information. Organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, and mitigation guidance is available on the CISA website. Mitsubishi Electric recommends installing the latest update to minimize the risk of exploitation. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8.
