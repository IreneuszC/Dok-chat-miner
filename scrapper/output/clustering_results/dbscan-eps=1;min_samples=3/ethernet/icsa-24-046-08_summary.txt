Siemens provides industrial security recommendations in their product manuals and security advisory SSA-665034. They recommend protecting network access to devices and configuring the IT environment according to their guidelines. A vulnerability in the WebSocket channel used for the Check Point IoT integration in Nozomi Networks Guardian and CMC has been reported, and Siemens has notified CISA. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
