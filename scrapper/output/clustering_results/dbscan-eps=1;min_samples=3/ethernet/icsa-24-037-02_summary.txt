HID Global has reported a vulnerability in their iCLASS SE reader configuration cards, which could allow sensitive data to be extracted. They recommend updating readers and credentials with new keys and implementing defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA has assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.3 to the vulnerability and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely.
