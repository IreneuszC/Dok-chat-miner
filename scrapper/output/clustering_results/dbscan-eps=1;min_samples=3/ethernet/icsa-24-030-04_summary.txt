CISA has issued a warning about vulnerabilities in Hitron Systems DVRs that could be exploited by attackers. They recommend implementing cybersecurity strategies and following mitigation guidance. The vulnerabilities could allow for denial-of-service attacks using default admin credentials. Hitron Systems has released updates to address the vulnerabilities. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.4 has been calculated for the vulnerabilities. Organizations are encouraged to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
