CISA recommends taking defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in HID products, including hardening reader and encoder configurations, protecting credentials, and disabling legacy technologies. The vulnerability could expose sensitive data and allow an attacker to read data from reader configuration cards and credentials. No known public exploitation has been reported, and the vulnerability is not exploitable remotely. HID Global reported the vulnerability to CISA and additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage. Organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
