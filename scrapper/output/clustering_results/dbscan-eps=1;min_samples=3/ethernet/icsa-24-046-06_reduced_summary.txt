Siemens has identified a vulnerability in their products that could allow an attacker to gain SYSTEM privileges. To reduce the risk, Siemens recommends protecting network access to devices with appropriate mechanisms and configuring the environment according to their security recommendations. CISA recommends users to take defensive measures and implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation of this vulnerability. No known public exploitation specifically targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely.
