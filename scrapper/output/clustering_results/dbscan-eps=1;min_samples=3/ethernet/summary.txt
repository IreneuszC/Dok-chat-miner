Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in various industrial products from companies such as Rockwell Automation, Mitsubishi Electric, Crestron, Siemens, HID Global, Delta Electronics, AVEVA, Chirp Systems, APsystems, Hitron Systems, Westermo, and Lantronix. These vulnerabilities range from denial-of-service, memory overwriting, and remote code execution to weak file and folder permissions, lack of proper authentication, and unauthorized access. CISA has been informed of these vulnerabilities and recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following established procedures for reporting suspected malicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to take defensive measures and implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage and in product manuals.
