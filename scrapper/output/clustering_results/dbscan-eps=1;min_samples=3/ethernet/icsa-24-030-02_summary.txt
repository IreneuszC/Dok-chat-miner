Mitsubishi Electric has reported vulnerabilities in their FA Engineering Software Products, which could allow remote attackers to bypass authentication, disclose, tamper with, destroy, or delete information, cause denial-of-service conditions, and execute malicious code. The company recommends mitigation measures to minimize the risk of exploitation and encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
