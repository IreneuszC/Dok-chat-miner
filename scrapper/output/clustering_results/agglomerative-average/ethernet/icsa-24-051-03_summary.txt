Mitsubishi Electric has reported a vulnerability in Microsoft Message Queuing service affecting their electrical discharge machines, which could allow attackers to execute remote code, disclose or tamper with information, or cause a denial-of-service condition. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage, and users are encouraged to install the latest update from Mitsubishi Electric to minimize the risk of exploitation. The vulnerability has been assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 9.8.
