Mitsubishi Electric has reported an authentication bypass vulnerability in the MELSEC WS Series Ethernet Interface Modules to CISA. The vulnerability has a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.9 and could allow remote attackers to login and tamper with programs and parameters. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
