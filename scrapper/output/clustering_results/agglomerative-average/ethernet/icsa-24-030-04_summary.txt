CISA has issued a warning about vulnerabilities in Hitron Systems DVRs that could be exploited by attackers. They recommend implementing cybersecurity strategies and following mitigation guidance available on the ICS webpage. The vulnerability allows for a denial-of-service attack using default admin credentials. Hitron Systems has released updates for affected DVR versions. A CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.4 has been calculated for the vulnerability. Organizations are advised to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
