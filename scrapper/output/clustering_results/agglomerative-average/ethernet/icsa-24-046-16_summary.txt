CISA is urging organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies to mitigate a privilege escalation vulnerability in FactoryTalk Service Platform (FTSP) reported by Rockwell Automation. The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could allow malicious users to gain administrator privileges, potentially leading to data manipulation and system unavailability. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Defensive measures and risk mitigations are recommended to minimize the risk of exploitation.
