Siemens provides industrial security recommendations in their product manuals, including protecting network access and configuring the IT environment. A vulnerability in the WebSocket channel used for the Check Point IoT integration in Nozomi Networks Guardian and CMC has been reported, and Siemens has recommended defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. Additional information and mitigation guidance are available on the ICS webpage.
