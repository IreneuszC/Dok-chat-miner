APsystems ENERGY COMMUNICATION UNIT (ECU-C) Power Control Software V4.1NA V3.11.4 W2.1NA V4.1SAA C1.2.2 has a vulnerability that allows attackers to access sensitive data and execute commands with full admin rights. APsystems has not responded to requests to mitigate the vulnerability, and users are encouraged to take defensive measures. CISA has assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 8.8 to the vulnerability and recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting the vulnerability has been reported to CISA at this time.
