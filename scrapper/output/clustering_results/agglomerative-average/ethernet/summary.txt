The text contains a comprehensive list of vulnerabilities found in various products and systems, including Siemens, Rockwell Automation, Mitsubishi Electric, Crestron, and others. The vulnerabilities range from denial-of-service attacks to remote code execution and privilege escalation. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and following mitigation guidance to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are advised to report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional information and mitigation guidance are available on the ICS webpage and the respective company websites.
