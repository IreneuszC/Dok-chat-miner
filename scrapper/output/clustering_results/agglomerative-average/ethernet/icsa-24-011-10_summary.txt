Siemens has issued a security advisory for the vulnerability in maxView Storage Manager on Windows. They recommend protecting network access and configuring the IT environment according to their recommendations. Siemens has identified specific workarounds and mitigations for reducing risk. The vulnerability has been reported to CISA and a CVSS v3 base score of 10.0 has been assigned. CISA recommends defensive measures and organizations should report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
