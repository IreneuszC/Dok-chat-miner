Siemens has reported a vulnerability in the Treck TCP/IP stack on certain devices to CISA. The vulnerability could allow unauthenticated remote attackers to cause a denial of service or execute remote code. No known public exploitation has been reported, but the vulnerability has a high attack complexity. Siemens has provided workarounds and mitigations, and CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. Additional information and guidance are available on the ICS webpage and Siemens' industrial security resources.
