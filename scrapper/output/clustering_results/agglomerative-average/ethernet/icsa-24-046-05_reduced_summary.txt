Siemens reported a vulnerability to CISA, which could allow an unauthenticated remote attacker to gain full administrative access to the application. Siemens recommends protecting network access with appropriate mechanisms and configuring the environment according to their recommendations. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of this vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
