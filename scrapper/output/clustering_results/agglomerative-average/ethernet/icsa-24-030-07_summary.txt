Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in Rockwell Automation products, with base scores ranging from 6.5 to 8.8. These vulnerabilities can be exploited through specifically crafted communication requests, leading to denial-of-service conditions, memory overwriting, or remote code execution. Rockwell Automation has recommended risk mitigations and encourages users to apply them. CISA has been notified of these vulnerabilities and recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are advised to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
