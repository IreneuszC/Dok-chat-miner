A Denial-of-Service (DoS) vulnerability has been identified in Rockwell Automation ControlLogix and GuardLogix programmable logic controllers, which could potentially cause a major nonrecoverable fault (MNRF) and crash the device. Rockwell Automation has reported the vulnerability to CISA and recommends users to apply risk mitigations and security best practices. CISA advises organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity for tracking and correlation against other incidents. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
