Siemens has issued a security advisory for a vulnerability in the maxView Storage Manager on Windows. The vulnerability could allow unauthorized access to the system. Siemens recommends implementing appropriate security measures and following their recommendations in product manuals. They have also provided specific workarounds and mitigations to reduce the risk. CISA has been notified of the vulnerability and recommends defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies.
