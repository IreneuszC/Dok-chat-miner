CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in Hitron Systems DVR. They have received reports of active exploitation and have assigned a CVSS v3.1 base score of 7.4 to the vulnerabilities. Hitron Systems has released updates for affected DVR versions. Aline Eliovich, Chad Seaman, and Larry Cashdollar of Akamai Technologies reported the vulnerabilities to CISA. Organizations should follow internal procedures and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
