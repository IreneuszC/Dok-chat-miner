Mitsubishi Electric has reported a denial-of-service vulnerability in their FA products to CISA. They recommend specific mitigation measures to minimize the risk of exploitation, and encourage organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity. The vulnerability affects certain versions of the MELSEC iQ-F Series and could be exploited by a remote attacker to cause a temporary denial-of-service condition in the product's Ethernet communication. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported at this time.
