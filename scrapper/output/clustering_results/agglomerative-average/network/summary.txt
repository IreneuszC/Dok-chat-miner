Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in various industrial control systems and products from companies such as Siemens, Rockwell Automation, Mitsubishi Electric, Crestron, and others. These vulnerabilities could allow attackers to execute code, gain unauthorized access, cause denial-of-service conditions, and perform other malicious actions. The companies have recommended specific mitigations and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. CISA has been informed of these vulnerabilities and recommends organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported at this time. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
