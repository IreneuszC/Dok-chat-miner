The article discusses vulnerabilities in Delta Electronics products, specifically CNCSoft-B versions 1.0.0.4 and prior, which could allow attackers to execute arbitrary code. The vulnerability is not exploitable remotely, but organizations are encouraged to follow established procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Recommended cybersecurity strategies and mitigation guidance are available on the ICS webpage. Users are advised to update to the latest version to minimize the risk of exploitation.
