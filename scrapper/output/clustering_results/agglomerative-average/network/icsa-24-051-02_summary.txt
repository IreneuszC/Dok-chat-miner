The Industrial Control Systems Network Protocol Parsers (ICSNPP) - Ethercat Zeek Plugin versions d78dda6 and prior are vulnerable to out-of-bounds read, write, and remote code execution. Users are advised to update the plugin and keep software up to date to reduce the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations should follow internal procedures and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
