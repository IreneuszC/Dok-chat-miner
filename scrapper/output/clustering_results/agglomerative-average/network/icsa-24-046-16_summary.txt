CISA recommends organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and mitigation guidance for a privilege escalation vulnerability in FactoryTalk Service Platform (FTSP). The vulnerability has a high attack complexity and could allow malicious users to gain administrator privileges, potentially compromising sensitive data and rendering the system unavailable. Rockwell Automation has reported the vulnerability to CISA and encourages users to apply risk mitigations. No known public exploitation targeting the vulnerability has been reported at this time.
