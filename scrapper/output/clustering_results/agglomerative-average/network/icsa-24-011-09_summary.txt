Siemens has identified vulnerabilities in their products that could allow attackers to gain unauthorized access and control of devices. They recommend implementing security measures and following their product manuals for protection. Siemens has reported the vulnerabilities to CISA and provided workarounds to reduce the risk. No known public exploitation has been reported, but organizations are encouraged to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage.
