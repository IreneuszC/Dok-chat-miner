Emerson Rosemount GC370XA, GC700XA, and GC1500XA products have vulnerabilities that could allow unauthorized access to sensitive information or cause a denial-of-service condition. To minimize the risk of exploitation, users should update the affected products' firmware, implement cybersecurity best practices, and ensure the products are connected to a well-protected network. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
