CISA advises organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before implementing defensive measures. Users of affected versions of MOVITOOLS MotionStudio are urged to contact Esjay (@esj4y) for assistance with mitigating vulnerabilities. The product has a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.5 and can result in unrestricted file access when processing XML information. CISA offers cybersecurity best practices for organizations and encourages the implementation of recommended strategies. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage, and organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
