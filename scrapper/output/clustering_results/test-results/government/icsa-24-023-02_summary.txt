CISA advises organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures. They recommend taking defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of a vulnerability in Crestron AM-300 firmware version 1.4499.00018. The vulnerability has been resolved in firmware version 1.4499.00023.001 or higher. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. CISA encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity to them for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
