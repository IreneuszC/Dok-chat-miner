CISA has issued advisories for multiple vulnerabilities in various products, including Crestron, Schneider Electric, SystemK, Lynx, APsystems, AVEVA, Horner Automation, MOVITOOLS MotionStudio, ViewPower Pro, Integration Objects, XPort, and Siemens. They recommend conducting impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures, and encourage organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity to CISA. Mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage for each vulnerability. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and CISA offers additional mitigation guidance and best practices for affected users.
