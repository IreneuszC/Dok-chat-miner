The ViewPower Pro UPS management software is vulnerable to OS command injection, unauthenticated user account modification, and deserialization of untrusted data, with potential for remote code execution and denial-of-service attacks. Voltronic Power did not respond to CISA's coordination attempts, and users are advised to contact the company for updates. CISA recommends implementing defensive measures and cybersecurity strategies to mitigate the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations should report any suspected malicious activity to CISA. Additional mitigation guidance and best practices are available on the ICS webpage.
