Schneider Electric customers should use appropriate patching methodologies and backups when applying patches to their systems. They have released mitigations and fixes for vulnerabilities in their products, and recommend evaluating the impact of patches in a test environment. CISA recommends performing impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures. No known public exploitation of the vulnerabilities has been reported, and CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies and report any suspected malicious activity.
