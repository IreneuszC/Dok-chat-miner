CISA has issued a warning about a vulnerability in Horner Automation Cscape software versions 9.90 SP10 and prior, which could allow local attackers to execute arbitrary code. They recommend performing impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures, and provide guidance on minimizing the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation of the vulnerability has been reported, and CISA encourages organizations to implement cybersecurity strategies and report any suspicious activity. Additional mitigation guidance is available on the ICS webpage.
