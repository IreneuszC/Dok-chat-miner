CISA recommends organizations to perform impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in MOVITOOLS MotionStudio. SEW-EURODRIVE has not responded to requests to mitigate these vulnerabilities. Users are encouraged to contact Esjay working with Trend Micro Zero Day Initiative for more information. The vulnerability could result in unrestricted file access and has a CVSS v3.1 base score of 5.5. CISA offers cyber defense best practices and encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. Organizations observing suspicious activity should report findings to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
