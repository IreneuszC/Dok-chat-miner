Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in the Lynx 206-F2G layer three industrial Ethernet switch, allowing attackers to introduce arbitrary JavaScript, execute malicious code, obtain sensitive information, and craft malicious requests. The vulnerabilities have been assigned CVSS v3.1 base scores ranging from 5.4 to 8.0. CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies, performing impact analysis and risk assessment, and following best practices for hardening to mitigate the reported vulnerabilities. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, and organizations are encouraged to report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
