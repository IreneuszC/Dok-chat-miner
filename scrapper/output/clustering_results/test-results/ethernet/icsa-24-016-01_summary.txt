CISA advises organizations to conduct impact analysis and risk assessment before implementing defensive measures. They recommend taking defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation of vulnerabilities in MOVITOOLS MotionStudio. SEW-EURODRIVE has not worked with CISA to mitigate these vulnerabilities. Esjay reported the vulnerability to CISA, which could result in unrestricted file access when processing XML information. The CVSS v3.1 base score for this vulnerability is 5.5. CISA offers cyber defense best practices for organizations and encourages the implementation of recommended cybersecurity strategies. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported, and it is not exploitable remotely. Mitigation guidance and recommended practices are available on the ICS webpage. Organizations should report suspected malicious activity to CISA for tracking and correlation against other incidents.
