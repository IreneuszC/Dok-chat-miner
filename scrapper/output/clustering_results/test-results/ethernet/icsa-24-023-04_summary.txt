Multiple vulnerabilities have been identified in the Lynx 206-F2G industrial Ethernet switch, allowing attackers to introduce arbitrary JavaScript, execute malicious code, obtain sensitive information, and craft malicious requests. The vulnerabilities have been assigned CVSS v3.1 base scores and CISA recommends implementing cybersecurity strategies and defensive measures to minimize the risk of exploitation. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported, but organizations are advised to follow established procedures and report any suspected malicious activity to CISA.
