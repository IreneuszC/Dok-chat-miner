CISA reminds organizations to perform impact analysis and risk assessment before deploying defensive measures. They recommend upgrading to newer versions of XPort to minimize the risk of exploitation. A vulnerability has been assigned to this issue with a base score of 5.7. Weakly encoded credentials in web request headers could allow attackers to obtain credentials. CISA encourages implementing recommended cybersecurity strategies and offers guidance on protecting against social engineering attacks. No known public exploitation targeting this vulnerability has been reported to CISA at this time.
