The ViewPower Pro UPS management software is vulnerable to OS command injection, unauthenticated user method invocation, and deserialization of untrusted data, leading to remote code execution, denial-of-service, and obtaining administrator credentials. Voltronic Power did not respond to CISA's coordination attempts, and users are encouraged to contact the company for updates. CISA recommends performing impact analysis and risk assessment, implementing defensive measures, and following established procedures for suspected malicious activity. Additional mitigation guidance and best practices are available on the CISA website. No known public exploitation targeting these vulnerabilities has been reported.
